What is an Orthophoto?

An orthophoto is a drone image that has been corrected for tilt and perspective so every point is at a consistent scale. Unlike a normal aerial photo, you can measure distances and areas directly on the image.

Why it matters to homeowners

  • Plan improvements: Measure decks, driveways, fences, and landscaping before you build.
  • Document your property: Keep a visual record of your roof, yard, and structures for insurance or resale.
  • See the whole picture: Understand how your home, yard, and neighboring lots relate in one clear view.

Typical deliverables

  • High-resolution orthophoto (GeoTIFF and web-friendly image)
  • Measurement overlays (areas, distances, annotations)
  • Optional web viewer for interactive zoom and pan
