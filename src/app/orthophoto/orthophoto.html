What Is an Orthophoto?

An orthophoto is a geometrically corrected aerial image where scale is uniform, allowing it to be used like a map. Perfect for measuring distances, areas, and documenting property conditions.

Use Cases

  • Property boundary visualization
  • Roof and structure documentation
  • Pre‑construction and post‑construction mapping
  • Insurance and appraisal support
