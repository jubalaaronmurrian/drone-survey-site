What is NDVI?

NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) uses near-infrared and visible light to measure how “alive” vegetation is. Healthy plants reflect light differently than stressed or dying plants, and NDVI turns that into a clear map.

Why it matters to homeowners

  • Find hidden stress: Spot areas where grass or trees are struggling before they visibly brown out.
  • Optimize irrigation: See where you’re over-watering or under-watering and adjust sprinklers accordingly.
  • Protect landscaping investments: Catch issues early on expensive trees, hedges, or garden beds.

Typical deliverables

  • Colorized NDVI map (red/yellow/green for vegetation health)
  • Side-by-side NDVI and regular aerial imagery
  • Summary report highlighting problem zones and recommendations
