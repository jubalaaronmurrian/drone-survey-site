NDVI Vegetation Analysis

Multispectral drone mapping to evaluate vegetation health, irrigation performance, and land management conditions.

What NDVI Reveals

NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) highlights plant health by measuring how vegetation reflects near‑infrared and visible light.

Applications

  • Landscape health monitoring
  • Irrigation and drainage issues
  • Crop stress detection
  • Environmental and land‑use studies

Deliverables

  • NDVI colorized maps
  • Raw multispectral data (optional)
  • Vegetation health reports
  • Side‑by‑side comparison with RGB imagery
