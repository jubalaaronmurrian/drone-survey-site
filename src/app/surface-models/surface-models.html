What are surface and elevation models?

A surface model is a 3D representation of your property, including ground, structures, and vegetation. From this, we can generate elevation maps, contour lines, and slope analysis.

Why it matters to homeowners

  • Drainage and flooding: See where water naturally collects and flows during heavy rain.
  • Plan grading and hardscapes: Design patios, driveways, and retaining walls with the slope in mind.
  • Avoid costly surprises: Identify low spots, steep areas, or problematic slopes before construction.

Typical deliverables

  • Elevation heatmap of your property
  • Contour lines overlaid on an orthophoto
  • 3D visualization or point cloud (for advanced planning)
